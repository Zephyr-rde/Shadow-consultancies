About Us

We Guide Your Educational Journey

Shadow Consultancies was founded with a singular mission: to make college admissions stress-free for 2nd PUC students in Bangalore. We understand the challenges and anxieties that come with this pivotal life decision.

With our deep expertise and special partnership with Marvadi University in Gujarat, we offer unique opportunities that other consultancies simply can't match.

Our team of dedicated education experts works tirelessly to match each student with the perfect college for their academic goals and personal growth.

We believe that every student deserves personalized guidance, transparent advice, and extra perks that make their college journey smoother—from free flight tickets to placement support after graduation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What makes Shadow Consultancies different from other education consultants?

We offer exclusive benefits like free flight tickets, direct partnerships with prestigious universities, and continued support even after admission. Our personalized approach ensures each student receives guidance tailored to their unique needs.

How does the free flight ticket program work?

Students who secure admission to Marvadi University through our consultancy receive a complimentary round-trip flight ticket to help them travel to the campus. This is our way of easing the transition to university life.

What kind of placement support do you provide?

Our placement support includes resume building workshops, interview preparation, career counseling, and connections to our extensive network of corporate partners. We maintain relationships with students throughout their academic journey.

Do you help with scholarships and financial aid?

Yes! We provide comprehensive guidance on available scholarships, help you identify which ones you qualify for, and assist with the application process to maximize your chances of receiving financial support.
