How It Works

Our simple yet effective process from waste to fertilizer

1

Waste Collection

We collect food waste from homes, restaurants, and supermarkets using our eco-friendly vehicles and specialized containers.

2

Processing & Composting

At our facilities, waste is sorted and then processed through advanced composting techniques that optimize nutrient retention.

3

Fertilizer Production

The compost is refined, enriched, and packaged into various fertilizer products designed for different agricultural needs.
