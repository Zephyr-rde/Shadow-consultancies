About Us

Your trusted education partner in Bangalore

We Guide Your Educational Journey

Shadow Consultancies was founded with a singular mission: to make college admissions stress-free for 2nd PUC students in Bangalore. We understand the challenges and anxieties that come with this pivotal life decision.

With our deep expertise and special partnership with Marvadi University in Gujarat, we offer unique opportunities that other consultancies simply can't match. Our team of dedicated education experts works tirelessly to match each student with the perfect college for their academic goals and personal growth.

We believe that every student deserves personalized guidance, transparent advice, and extra perks that make their college journey smoother—from free flight tickets to placement support after graduation.
